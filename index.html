Nunes Exits Congress, Sticks County With Big Election Tab

Rep. Devin Nunes, R-Tulare officially stepped down Monday to become CEO of former President Donald Trump’s startup social media company. Fresno County Clerk James Kus estimates that holding special elections to replace Nunes will cost local taxpayers up to $1.3 million.

Photos Show Beloved Betty White Visiting Fresno Chaffee Zoo

The late actress, and renowned advocate for animals, was on hand for the zoo’s 50th anniversary “Zoobilee” in 1979. Linda Cover, who is currently the park’s animal registrar, gave White a tour and shared her photos and recollections of the memorable day.

Top CA Court Rejects Early Releases for Violent Offenders

The state Supreme Court unanimously ruled Monday that corrections officials need not consider earlier release for violent felons, even those whose primary offense is considered nonviolent under state law. The ruling upheld other appellate decisions that supported the tighter regulations.
